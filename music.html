Some Music Projects I worked on...

The Ritual Staff: A New Digital Instrument

This is an instrument that I built inspired by ritual dances and related appliances from different cultures. The idea is that the performer is collaborating with the spirit in the staff when they perform the instrument. The staff has a IMU which can pick up its motion information. I then processed these information using Max, connecting different movements to different tones. The can on the staff is a trigger that acts as a record button. The performer can record a certain length of the notes played and have them played back in loops, and the ghost will alter the played audio by adding different effects if you "disobey" its instructions.



The Treee Factory

In this project, I built a musical installation by hosting a webpage with all the different tree photos that I took within UM campus. These photos are kept as unique copies online, and everyone could print out any one of these photos. But if a photo is printed, the file will be removed from the website, and therefore the physical copy of the printed photo will be the only copy of this tree. An individual can then scan the photo with the provided scanner to produce music patterns that is generated by processing the photo's visual data.
